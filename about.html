History about Nike

SwooshWear started in 2022 by sports enthusiasts wanting high-quality gear that works as hard as you do. From garage prototypes to full collections, we've grown but kept our core philosophy intact.

Vision of the Company

To become the brand people trust for gear that performs, inspires, and lasts — from gym sessions to daily life.

Our Mission

Design innovative activewear that encourages movement, encourages confidence, and adapts for everyone. Sustainability is fundamental — we prioritize recycled materials and ethical manufacturing.
